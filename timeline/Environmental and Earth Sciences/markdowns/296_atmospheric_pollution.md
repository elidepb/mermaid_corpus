        1980s : Catalytic converters required in vehicles

    section Global Awareness
        2000s : Particulate matter health impacts recognized
        2010s : Air quality monitoring networks expand
    section Current Era
        2020s : PM2.5 & ultrafine particles focus
        2020s : Global air quality standards & WHO guidelines
