```mermaid
timeline
    title The History of 3D Printing
    section The 1980s: The Birth of Additive Manufacturing
        1981 : Hideo Kodama's rapid prototyping system
        1986 : Chuck Hull patents Stereolithography (SLA)
        1987 : Carl Deckard patents Selective Laser Sintring (SLS)
        1989 : S. Scott Crump patents Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)
    section The 1990s: Commercialization and Growth
        1990s : The first commercial 3D printers are sold
        1999 : The first 3D-printed organ (a bladder) is implanted
    section The 2000s: The Open Source Revolution
        2005 : The RepRap Project, a self-replicating 3D printer, is founded
        2009 : MakerBot is founded
    section The 2010s: The Age of the Consumer
        2010s : Expiration of key patents leads to a boom in consumer 3D printing
        2014 : 3D printing is used to create a prosthetic jaw
    section The 2020s: The Industrial Revolution
        2020s : 3D printing is increasingly used for mass production
        2020s : Advancements in metal 3D printing and bioprinting
```
