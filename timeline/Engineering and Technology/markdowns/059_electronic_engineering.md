        1971: The first microprocessor (Intel 4004)
        1970s: Development of microcontrollers
    section: The 1980s-1990s - The Digital and Wireless Eras
        1980s: Rise of personal computers and digital electronics
        1990s: Proliferation of wireless communication (e.g., cell phones, Wi-Fi)
    section: The 21st Century - Miniaturization and Integration
        2000s: System on a Chip (SoC)
        2010s: Internet of Things (IoT) and wearable electronics
