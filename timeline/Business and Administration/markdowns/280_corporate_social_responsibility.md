    title The History of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

        1991 : Carroll's Pyramid of CSR

        2000 : UN Global Compact launched
        2010 : ISO 26000 guidance on social responsibility
        2015 : Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
        2020s : Rise of ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance)
