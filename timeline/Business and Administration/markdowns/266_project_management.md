        1950s : Critical Path Method (CPM) & PERT developed

        1969 : Project Management Institute (PMI) founded

        1984 : The Goal by Eliyahu Goldratt - Theory of Constraints
        1996 : PRINCE2 methodology is published
    section 21st Century
        2001 : The Agile Manifesto is published
        2010s : Rise of Hybrid methodologies & Digital PM tools
